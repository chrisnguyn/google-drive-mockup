About Me

Hello, world!

Who am I?

My name is Christopher Nguyen. I'm currently a senior Computer Science student studying at York University. I've done two prior internships (MLH Fellowship, CaseWare), doing a third this upcoming winter (MDA Space), and would love to have Shopify be my fourth this summer.

Historically, most of my experience has been in backend development. My projects are all largely backend related and my first internship was a Python focused backend role in open source. My second internship was primarily in frontend development, and I'm happy for the opportunity to experience both disciplines in a professional environment.


Why Shopify?

Ever since I was a first year university student, I've always been very interested in working at Shopify. I think that Shopify's mission of trying to make e-commerce better for everyone is an especially challenging problem to solve.

Providing people with the ability to become an entrepreneur regardless of your background is especially important and powerful. My sister has a store powered by the Shopify platform - no programming or financial expertise needed. Just an idea and motivation to make it happen - and Shopify takes care of all the technical work in the background such as setting up the store and payment system, allowing merchants to fully focus on their merchandise.

Enabling people with the ability to be become an entrepreneur regardless of prior experience, formal education, and background is incredible. I want to be part of this journey to make e-commerce better for everyone.


Why me?

I am crazy motivated about things I am passionate about, and Shopify is a company that puts a lot of emphasis on enabling people to start their own business. I've always helped Shopify at such a high esteem, and will continuously keep getting inspiring by the good Shopify does.

I'm in my last year of my studies and on my way to do my third internship - I want to use all the experiences I've garnered and things I've learned and make a large impact at Shopify.
